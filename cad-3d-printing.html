CAD & 3D Printing Services

Precision design and rapid prototyping solutions

Professional 3D Design & Manufacturing

Bay State Engineering offers comprehensive CAD design and 3D printing services to bring your ideas from concept to reality. Our team of experienced designers and engineers utilize industry-leading software and cutting-edge additive manufacturing technology to deliver high-quality results.

Whether you need a single prototype or a batch of functional parts, we have the expertise and equipment to meet your requirements with precision and efficiency.

What We Offer

🎨 3D CAD Modeling

Detailed product design using SolidWorks, Fusion 360, and AutoCAD. From simple parts to complex assemblies.

📐 Technical Drawings

Complete engineering drawings with dimensions, tolerances, and specifications for manufacturing.

🔄 Reverse Engineering

3D scanning and CAD reconstruction of existing parts for reproduction or modification.

🖨️ FDM 3D Printing

Fused Deposition Modeling for functional prototypes and end-use parts in various materials.

✨ Resin Printing

High-resolution SLA/DLP printing for detailed models, miniatures, and precision parts.

⚙️ Post-Processing

Finishing services including sanding, painting, vapor smoothing, and assembly.

🔍 Design Optimization

Design for manufacturing (DFM) consultation to ensure parts are optimized for 3D printing.

📦 Small Batch Production

Low-volume manufacturing for custom parts and limited production runs.
