Who We Work With

Built for independent restaurants

We work exclusively with single-location restaurants — owner-operated, family-run, and small partnerships. Not chains, groups, or franchises.

Strong candidates for whom we can provide the most value typically have one or more of these characteristics:

✓

Inconsistent or missing digital presence

Your hours, address, or menu are wrong somewhere online — or you don't have a real web presence at all.

✓

Unaddressed online reviews

You have reviews — good or bad — sitting out there that you've never responded to.

✓

Nearby restaurants attract more customers

You know competitors with comparable (or worse) food are busier than you, and you're not sure why.

✓

Good foot traffic, low conversion

People walk by but don't come in — something about how you present yourself isn't landing.

✓

Food quality outperforms the experience

Your food is the best part of the meal, but the dining environment isn't living up to it.

✓

No one is looking at the numbers

Purchasing, suppliers, and recurring spend haven't been scrutinized — and there's likely savings to find.
